About Me -- Deleha Decker

Deleha Decker is a second year student at Syracuse University majoring in Graphic Design at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications and Information Management and Technology at the School of Information Studies. Upon receiving her Bachelor's Degree in May 2023, Deleha aspires to begin her own creative content digital platform to share her faith and outreach to others.

On campus, Deleha is an intern at Baptist Campus Ministry, a peer advisor for the iSchool, a SEM 100 peer facilitator and she actively serves with Syracuse Cru on both the Media and Leadership Teams. In her free time, Deleha can be found hanging out with friends, growing in her skills and faith and eating Oreos.
