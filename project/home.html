The Whosoever Church

We have a heart of compassion for people who are in need. We count it an honor to minister to everyone God leads to our doors. Our congregation cuts across all societal lines, including those on the streets, criminals, and drug addicts. We do not shy away from caring for those who are generally not welcomed or loved elsewhere. We don't look at color, wealth (or lack of), or anything else that may be a dividing wall in regular society. Our message of reconciliation to our Heavenly Father through Jesus Christ is for everyone, and that life need not be lived in despair and hopelessness. There is freedom in Christ! For who? Whosoever! Whatever condition that you're in, you're welcome here at the First Baptist Church.
