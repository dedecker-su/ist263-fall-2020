Our Beliefs

We believe... In only one God who is the creator of all things and being in the Trinity of the Father, Son, and Holy Ghost.
We believe...The Son of God, Jesus Christ, became incarnate, was begotten by the Holy Ghost, born of a virgin, and is the true God and true man.
We believe...The Bible, in it's entirety, to be the inspired Word of God and the only infallible rule of faith and conduct.
We believe... In the resurrection power of Jesus Christ, the eternal life of the saved, and the eternal punishment of the lost.
We believe... The only way of salvation is a personal relationship with Jesus Christ through His shed blood on the cross. (to be born again) Romans 10: 9,10 and John 3:3
We believe... In water baptism by immersion for believers according to Matthew 28:19
We believe... In divine healing through the power of Jesus Christ as stated in the Scriptures.
We believe... In the Holy Ghost by Whom believers of Jesus Christ are regenerated, baptized into Christ, indwelt, sealed, and filled for service.
We believe... In the Christian's hope and the imminent personal return of the Lord Jesus Christ.

As the Senior Pastor of the First Baptist Church, I would like to personally extend an open invitation to whoever, regardless of wherever you come from or whatever condition you are in.
